About Me

Dedicated cybersecurity professional committed to securing digital infrastructure

Professional Summary

I am a dedicated cybersecurity professional with over a year of hands-on experience in Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT), network security, and offensive security operations. My journey in cybersecurity began with a strong passion for ethical hacking and has evolved into practical expertise through real-world engagements.

During my 3-month internship at Demmisto Technologies, I conducted comprehensive security assessments on multiple live web applications, identifying critical vulnerabilities and providing actionable remediation strategies. This experience, combined with extensive freelance security projects, has equipped me with the skills to navigate complex security challenges in diverse environments.

I specialize in web application security testing, network vulnerability assessments, and exploit development using industry-standard tools including Burp Suite, Nmap, Wireshark, and OWASP ZAP. My approach is grounded in established frameworks such as ISO 27001, NIST Cybersecurity Framework, and MITRE ATT&CK, ensuring that my assessments align with industry best practices.

What Drives Me

My passion lies in adversarial security research and the continuous pursuit of knowledge in the ever-evolving cybersecurity landscape. I believe that staying ahead of emerging threats requires not just technical proficiency, but also a deep understanding of attacker methodologies and defense strategies.

I am committed to delivering scalable, secure solutions that protect organizations from both current and future threats. Whether it's identifying zero-day vulnerabilities or hardening existing systems, I approach each challenge with meticulous attention to detail and a goal-oriented mindset.
